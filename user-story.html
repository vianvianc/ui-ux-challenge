User Story

User Story: As a user, Ella is trying to get detailed information about the weather beyond the simple details found on most weather apps. In the summer for climbing she wants to know the hourly weather, humidity, and percent cloud cover to determine optimal conditions for trying her climbs. In the winter she wants a detailed snow forecast and uses the resorts webpage. She wants to use wunderground.com to get the most detailed information possible to decide where to go climb and if when conditions will be optimal.
